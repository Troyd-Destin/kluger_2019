The Founder

Dr. Ophelia Jarligo the founder and CEO of Klüger-Tech has 20+ years of hands-on experience in the study of the structures, thermal, physical and chemical properties of various materials. She holds a PhD in Materials Processing, MS in Materials Science and Engineering and BS in Ceramic Engineering. At Tohoku University (Japan), after her doctoral studies she landed a postdoc position and was involved in various activities related to micro-systems materials processing. She was then hired as a Scientist at Forschungszentrum Jülich (Germany) and worked on a wide range of energy materials related topics including the development, synthesis and processing of advanced thermal barrier coatings of gas turbines, ion transport membranes, solid oxide fuel cells and solid-state batteries. For more than six years at Jülich, Dr. Jarligo was involved in numerous EU and industry funded projects which worked on increasing operation efficiencies of energy conversion systems. In 2013, she was hired by the University of Alberta (Canada) through the Helmholz-Alberta Initiative (HAI) and managed the Membrane Development Team.

Dr. Jarligo is a permanent resident of Canada and has gained several years of Canadian industrial experience working on nano-technology, specialized surface systems and energy related fields to advance technologies to commercialization. She has 40+ scientific articles in journals and conference proceedings and contributed for the approval of two patents.
